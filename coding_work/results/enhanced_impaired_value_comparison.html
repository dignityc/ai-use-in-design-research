Overall Dimension x Value Balance

Insight

Background research is the largest value arena, but its benefits are mixed: efficiency rises while accuracy, context appropriateness, and trust are impaired. Planning and coordination show the strongest positive balance, suggesting that AI is especially valuable for speeding and coordinating design work.

Insight

Design phases show different value trade-off profiles. Discover and Define carry the strongest efficiency gains but also accuracy and context risks, while Develop concentrates creativity, feasibility, and scalability gains alongside trust and authenticity concerns.
